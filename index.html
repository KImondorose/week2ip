What are Akan names?

AKAN names are from the Ghanaian culture Children in Ghana are given a first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here is a table that illustrates the name:

# DAY MALE FEMALE
1 Sunday Kwasi Akosua
2 Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
3 Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
4 Wednesday Kwaku Akua
5 Thursday Yaw Yaa
6 Friday Kofi Afua
7 Saturday Kwame Ama
